# Competitor Scan

Read `projects/<project-slug>/strategy/intake.md`. Use web search if available;
otherwise reason from knowledge and label uncertainty explicitly.

## Output
1. **Map**: direct competitors, indirect alternatives, and the status quo
   ("do nothing / spreadsheet") — the status quo is usually the real enemy at
   early stage; say so when true.
2. Per key competitor: who they serve, positioning one-liner, pricing (if known),
   apparent strengths, exploitable weaknesses, what their existence PROVES about
   the market (validation vs. saturation).
3. **Gap analysis**: underserved segments, unclaimed positioning angles, pricing gaps.
   For any competitor with an App Store / Play Store presence: scan 1-star reviews
   (what users hate most, unmet needs) and 3-star reviews (what's tolerated but
   unsatisfying) — these are the most unfiltered signal of exploitable weakness.
   Label inferred gaps vs. directly evidenced ones.
4. **Strategic implications**: 2-3 options for how to play it (avoid/flank/attack)
   with pros/cons, one recommendation.

Rules: distinguish verified facts from inference. A crowded market is evidence of
demand, not only a threat — weigh both.
Save to `projects/<project-slug>/strategy/memos/competitors-vN.json`.
