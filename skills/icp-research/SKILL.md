# ICP Research

Read `projects/<project-slug>/strategy/intake.md` + latest assessment's icp_hypotheses.

## Deliver three things
1. **Segment decision memo**: 2-3 candidate segments with pros/cons on: pain
   intensity, reachability with THIS user's resources, willingness to pay,
   market size. One recommended beachhead. Beachhead = narrow enough that 10
   members would recognize each other.
2. **Where to find 10 of them this week**: specific communities, subreddits,
   LinkedIn filters, events, personal-network angles — tied to the user's
   unfair advantages from intake.
3. **Interview guide**: 8-10 questions about their current behavior and past
   actions, not opinions about your idea ("walk me through the last time you…",
   never "would you use…"). Include the 2-3 answers that would validate or
   kill the assumption.

Save to `projects/<project-slug>/strategy/memos/icp-vN.json`.
